Freight Jargon Decoded

Terms every shipper should know

NMFC Code

National Motor Freight Classification — determines your freight class

DIM Weight

Dimensional weight — carriers charge based on size, not just weight

Accessorial

Extra charges for services like liftgate, delivery app't, residential

BOL

Bill of Lading — your shipping contract and receipt

FSC

Fuel Surcharge — percentage added based on diesel prices

LTL

Less Than Truckload — shipments under 15,000 lbs
