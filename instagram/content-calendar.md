## Content Repurposing Strategy

| Instagram | LinkedIn | Twitter/X | YouTube |
|-----------|----------|-----------|---------|
| Carousel post | Long-form article | Thread | Tutorial video |
| Reel | Short post | Tweet + clip | Shorts |
| Story | — | Tweet | Community post |
| Feed post | Article | Thread | — |

### Cross-Posting Rules
1. Post on Instagram first
2. Adapt for LinkedIn (more professional tone)
3. Summarize for Twitter/X (thread format)
4. Repurpose reels as YouTube Shorts
5. Never copy-paste — always adapt for platform
