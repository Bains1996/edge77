DIM Weight Mistakes
Cost You 20-40%

Carriers calculate dimensional weight to charge you more. Here's how to catch them.

The DIM Weight Formula

Divide L × W × H (in inches) by the DIM factor (usually 139 for LTL). Compare to actual weight — carriers charge the HIGHER of the two.

DIM Weight = (L × W × H) ÷ 139
