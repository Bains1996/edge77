## 1. Step-by-Step Registration Guide

Capterra does NOT require a business email (unlike G2). Personal emails (Gmail, Outlook) work for initial registration.

### Step 1: Create a Vendor Account
1. Go to [capterra.com/register](https://www.capterra.com/register)
2. Select **"I'm a software vendor"**
3. Enter:
   - Full name
   - Email address (any domain — personal accepted)
   - Password
   - Company name: **EDGE77**
   - Company website: [your website URL]
4. Click **"Create Account"**
5. Verify email via confirmation link

### Step 2: Claim or Create Your Product Profile
1. Search Capterra for "EDGE77" to check if a profile already exists
2. If no profile exists, click **"Add your product"**
3. If a profile exists, click **"Claim this profile"** (you'll need to verify ownership)

### Step 3: Complete the Vendor Verification
Capterra requires phone verification (this is the main gate, not business email):
1. Enter your phone number
2. You'll receive an automated call with a verification code
3. Enter the code to proceed

### Step 4: Complete Product Profile Sections
Capterra requires these sections before listing goes live:

| Section | Status | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Product name + URL | Required | EDGE77 |
| Category selection | Required | See Section 3 |

### Step 5: Submit for Review
1. Click **"Submit for Review"** once all sections are complete

## 2. Product Description (Optimized for Capterra Format)

### Product Name
EDGE77

### Tagline (Short, 1 line)
**AI-Powered Freight Invoice Audit — Catch Carrier Billing Errors Automatically**

### Short Description (2-3 sentences)
EDGE77 automates freight invoice auditing for 3PLs and freight brokers. The platform uses AI-powered OCR to extract data from any invoice format, validates every charge against your contracted carrier rates, and auto-generates disputes — recovering 3-5% of invoiced spend without manual effort.

### Long Description (Capterra allows rich text — use these sections with headers)

**How EDGE77 Works**
EDGE77 ingests freight invoices via email, upload, or API. Our AI extracts line-item data from any format — PDF, image, EDI, or scanned document — using advanced OCR tailored for freight documents. Each charge is validated against your contracted rates, service agreements, and accessorial fee schedules. Discrepancies are flagged and dispute documents are generated automatically.

**Why Freight Audit Matters**
Freight brokers and 3PLs lose 3–5% of gross revenue to carrier billing errors: duplicate invoices, accessorial overcharges, rate mismatches, and unapproved surcharges. Manual audit doesn't scale and most automation requires TMS integration. EDGE77 works with what you already have.

**Key Capabilities**
- **AI Invoice Extraction:** OCR trained on freight invoices — captures line items, charges, accessorials, and reference numbers
- **Contract Validation:** Import carrier rate agreements and validate every charge automatically
- **Automated Dispute Generation:** Create professional dispute documents with supporting evidence in one click
- **Samsara Integration:** Cross-reference telematics data with detention and accessorial charges
- **Dashboard & Reporting:** Real-time visibility into audit findings, recovery rates, and carrier performance

**Who It's For**
- Freight brokers with 500+ invoices/month
- 3PLs managing multiple carrier relationships
- Logistics operations teams overwhelmed by manual audit
- Finance/AP teams seeking margin recovery

**Pricing**
- **Starter:** $0/mo (contingency-based — we earn when you recover)
- **Growth:** $499/mo (for teams with 1,000–5,000 invoices/month)
- **Enterprise:** $2,999/mo (custom integrations, dedicated support, API access)

## 7. Review Collection Strategy

### Why Reviews Matter on Capterra
- Each review increases your profile rank in search results
- Products with 10+ reviews get significantly more traffic
- Recent reviews (within 6 months) are weighted more heavily

### Phase 1: Initial Reviews (Weeks 1-4)
**Goal:** Get first 5 reviews to make profile look established

1. **Email existing beta customers** (personalized request)
2. **Offer incentive:** $50 Amazon gift card per review (check Capterra's current policy — typically allowed)
3. **Make it easy:** Send direct link to Capterra review form
4. **Target:** Ask customers who have had positive audit recoveries

**Email Template:**

```
Subject: Help us help the freight community — review EDGE77 on Capterra

Hi [Customer Name],

EDGE77 has helped [Company Name] recover [X] in carrier billing errors this year. We'd love your honest feedback on Capterra to help other brokers and 3PLs find the right audit solution.

Your review makes a difference — it helps our team improve and helps the industry discover better audit tools.

Review link (takes 3 minutes):
[Custom Capterra review link]

Thank you for being part of the EDGE77 community.

Best,
[Name]
```

### Phase 2: Growth Reviews (Month 2-6)
**Goal:** Reach 15-20 reviews

1. **Automated trigger:** Send review request 60 days after sign-up
2. **Segment:** Request reviews specifically from Growth + Enterprise users (Starter users may churn)
3. **In-app prompt:** Add non-blocking review prompt after a successful audit recovery
4. **Follow-up:** Send 2 reminders (1 week and 2 weeks after first request)

### Phase 3: Sustained Volume (Month 6+)
**Goal:** Maintain 2-3 new reviews per month

1. **Quarterly email blast** to active customers
2. **Review contest:** "Review of the Quarter" spotlight in newsletter
3. **Respond to every review** (positive and negative) within 48 hours
   - Positive: Thank them, mention what they specifically liked
   - Negative: Acknowledge, offer to resolve, show public accountability

### Review Response Guidelines
- **Always be professional** — never defensive
- **Thank the reviewer** for their time
- **Address specific points** mentioned in the review
- **For negative reviews:** Take responsibility, explain what's being done, invite them to connect
- **For positive reviews:** Reiterate your commitment, mention new features coming

### Capterra Review Policy Must-Knows
- ❌ Cannot pay for reviews (gift cards for time are OK, not for content)
- ❌ Cannot write your own reviews
- ❌ Cannot ask only happy customers (must invite all or none)
- ✅ Can segment by user cohort (e.g., customers who have been active 60+ days)
- ✅ Can offer incentives for time spent (not for positive content)
- ✅ Can respond publicly to any review
