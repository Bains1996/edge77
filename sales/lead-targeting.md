# EDGE77 Lead Targeting Guide

## Ideal Customer Profile (ICP)

| Attribute | Target |
|-----------|--------|
| **Company Size** | $10M–$500M annual revenue |
| **Industries** | Manufacturing, Retail, E-commerce, CPG, Food & Beverage, Automotive |
| **Shipping Volume** | 50+ invoices/month or $500K+ annual freight spend |
| **Pain Points** | Manual invoice review, missed overcharges, carrier billing disputes |
| **Decision Makers** | VP Operations, CFO, Director of Supply Chain, Logistics Manager |

---

## Lead Scoring Criteria

### A-Tier (Priority)
- 200+ invoices/month
- Enterprise WMS/ERP systems (SAP, Oracle, NetSuite, Manhattan)
- Dedicated logistics/transportation team
- Multiple carrier relationships
- Proactive cost-reduction initiatives

### B-Tier (Nurture)
- 50–200 invoices/month
- Some automation in place
- Growing shipping volume
- Active vendor evaluation cycle
- Recent leadership change in supply chain

### C-Tier (Low Priority)
- Fewer than 50 invoices/month
- Fully manual processes
- Small shipper profile
- Limited budget authority

## Where to Find Leads

### Digital Channels
- **LinkedIn Sales Navigator** — Filter by title (VP Ops, CFO, Dir. Supply Chain), company size, and industry
- **Freight industry publications** — Inbound Logistics, Supply Chain Dive, FreightWaves, JOC
- **State manufacturing associations** — Member directories for target states
- **Trade event attendee lists** — CSCMP EDGE, TIA Capital Ideas, FreightWaves LIVE

### Partnerships
- **Freight broker partnerships** — Co-sell with brokers whose clients need audit support
- **3PL relationships** — Referrals from 3PLs managing shippers in ICP range
- **ERP/WMS vendors** — Integration partnerships with Netsuite, SAP, Oracle

### Outbound Prospecting
- **Company trigger events** — New funding rounds, M&A activity, warehouse expansion, hiring logistics roles
- **Intent signals** — Companies searching for freight audit, parcel audit, or transportation cost solutions

## Outreach Cadence

### Week 1 — Cold Email Sequence
- **Day 1:** Initial outreach email (problem-focused, not product-focused)
- **Day 3:** Value-add follow-up (case study, stat, or insight)

### Week 2 — LinkedIn
- **Day 8:** Connection request with personalized note
- **Day 10:** Engage with their content (like, comment)

### Week 3 — Follow-Up Email
- **Day 15:** Follow-up email referencing the connection request
- **Day 17:** Break down the ROI with a quick calculator or example

### Week 4 — Phone Call
- **Day 22:** Direct phone call attempt
- **Day 24:** Voicemail + email follow-up

### Week 5 — Break-Up Email
- **Day 29:** Final "closing the loop" email with soft CTA

| Pain Point | Message |
|------------|---------|

## Qualification Questions

1. How many freight invoices do you process per month?
2. What's your approximate annual freight spend?
3. Do you currently use a freight audit provider?
4. What ERP/WMS system are you on?
5. Who on your team reviews carrier invoices today?
6. Have you identified overcharges in the past 12 months?
7. What's your biggest pain point with your current freight billing process?
