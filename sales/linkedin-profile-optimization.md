### Long Description (2,000+ characters)

**The Problem**
Up to 20% of freight invoices contain billing errors. Most brokerages audit manually — if they audit at all. They catch obvious duplicates but miss patterns: carriers who systematically overcharge, incorrect NMFC codes, fuel surcharge miscalculations that compound across thousands of shipments.

The result? 3-7% of freight spend leaking out in overcharges that look "normal" on the invoice.

**The Solution**
EDGE77 is an AI-powered freight invoice audit platform that detects billing errors at scale. Our engine is trained on millions of freight data points and identifies:

- Duplicate invoices (even when disguised with different numbers)
- Accessorial fee overcharges (liftgate, residential, inside delivery)
- Fuel surcharge miscalculations (wrong base rate, wrong MPG)
- Incorrect NMFC/class codes
- Carrier-specific overcharge patterns
- Minimum charge abuse
- Contract rate violations

**How It Works**
1. **Connect** — Upload invoices or connect via API to your TMS
2. **Parse** — AI extracts every line item from any format (CSV, EDI 210, PDF)
3. **Match** — Each charge is compared against your rate tables and contracts
4. **Detect** — Pattern recognition identifies systematic overcharging by carrier
5. **Report** — Prioritized discrepancies with line-by-line evidence

**The EDGE77 Difference**
- **$0/month to start** — No upfront fees, no annual contracts
- **Contingency pricing** — We only earn when you recover overcharges
- **Pattern detection** — AI learns carrier billing behaviors over time
- **All formats supported** — CSV, PDF, EDI 210, TMS exports
- **Fast setup** — From signup to first audit report in under 4 hours

**Our Stage**
We're early stage and proud of it. We're not pretending to be enterprise-grade SaaS with a 50-person sales team. We're a focused team building a focused product for freight brokerages that want to stop leaving money on the table.

**Who We Serve**
- Freight brokerages ($5M-$150M freight under management)
- 3PLs with multiple carrier relationships
- Logistics managers responsible for invoice accuracy
- Supply chain directors looking for cost recovery without headcount

## 5. Connection Growth Strategies

### Phase 1: Foundation (Weeks 1-4)

**Personal Account First**
- Have founders/team members optimize their personal profiles before pushing the company page
- Personal profiles drive 10x more engagement than company pages

**Targeted Connection Requests**
- Search LinkedIn for these titles:
  - "Freight Broker" + "Owner" or "Founder"
  - "3PL" + "CEO" or "President"
  - "Logistics Manager" + "Transportation"
  - "Supply Chain Director" + "Freight"
  - "VP Logistics" + "Operations"
- Send 20 connection requests/day with personalized notes (see DM templates in engagement strategy)

**Company Page Followers**
- Ask every new personal connection to follow the company page
- Add "Follow our company page EDGE77 for freight audit tips" to email signatures
- Embed follow button on website footer

### Phase 2: Growth (Weeks 5-8)

**Content Amplification**
- Team members share company posts with personal commentary (not just reshare — add 2-3 sentences of context)
- Tag 1-2 relevant connections in comments on company posts (not the post itself)
- Respond to every comment within 2 hours during business hours

**LinkedIn Groups**
- Join and participate in 5-10 logistics/freight groups (see engagement strategy for list)
- Share company posts to relevant groups (where allowed)
- Don't sell — add value with comments and then share relevant content

**Employee Advocacy**
- If >1 employee: create a content-sharing cadence
- Monday: share content to Slack/Teams with a suggested personal caption
- Friday: share weekly top performer (most engaged employee gets a shoutout)

### Phase 3: Scaling (Weeks 9-12)

**Influencer Collaboration**
- Comment on posts from logistics influencers (FreightWaves, Adam Robinson, etc.)
- Tag EDGE77 company page when adding valuable insight
- Build relationships before asking for anything

**Paid Follower Campaign**
- LinkedIn Sponsored Content with "Follow" objective
- Budget: $200-500/month to start
- Target: US-based, "Freight Broker" / "Logistics" / "Supply Chain" job titles
- Expected cost: $3-8 per follower
