## How It Works: 3 Steps

### 1. Upload
Invoices land in EDGE77 via email, API, or direct upload. We support any format — PDF, EDI, image, or structured data.

### 2. Audit
Our AI extracts every line item using OCR and validates each charge against the carrier's contract terms, fuel surcharge tables, and accessorial fee schedules. Samsara integration cross-references GPS data for distance-based charges.

### 3. Recover
Discrepancies are flagged in real time. EDGE77 generates carrier-ready dispute packets with supporting documentation. You approve, we send. Average recovery: **85% of billing errors.**

## Key Features

- **AI Extraction & OCR** — Reads invoices in any format, extracts 40+ data points per line item
- **Contract Rate Validation** — Ingests carrier rate tables and validates every charge against contracted terms
- **Fuel Surcharge Audit** — Validates FSC calculations against DOE/DOT index data and contract formulas
- **Accessorial Charge Detection** — Flags detention, lumper, stop-off, and re-weigh charges outside contract caps
- **Samsara Integration** — Cross-references odometer and GPS data to validate mileage-based charges
- **Automated Dispute Generation** — Creates complete dispute packets with contract references and supporting docs
- **Real-Time Dashboard** — Live recovery tracker, error classification, carrier performance metrics
- **Zero Implementation** — Deploy in 5–10 business days. No hardware, no IT project plan.
