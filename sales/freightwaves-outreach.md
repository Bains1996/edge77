## 2. Article Ideas (5 Topics)

### Topic 1: "The 3–5% Leak: Why Freight Audit Is the Highest-ROI Investment a Broker Can Make"

**Angle:** Educational/ROI-focused  
**Key points:**
- Industry stats on billing error rates (duplicates, overcharges, accessorial)
- Manual audit cost breakdown (labor hours, error rates, AP delays)
- How AI audit changes the ROI equation
- Case study estimates (real numbers, anonymized)
- Call to action: audit your last 100 invoices

### Topic 2: "OCR vs. API: Why Invoice Data Extraction Still Matters in a Connected World"

**Angle:** Technical/deep dive  
**Key points:**
- Most carrier invoices arrive as PDFs or images (not EDI)
- OCR accuracy benchmarks for freight invoices (vs. standard documents)
- When API integration works and when it doesn't
- How AI extraction + human review beats either alone
- The Samsara integration angle: combining telematics data with invoice validation

### Topic 3: "The Hidden Cost of Accessorial Charges: What Every 3PL Should Audit"

**Angle:** Practical/operational  
**Key points:**
- Most common accessorial overcharges (lumper, detention, re-weigh, etc.)
- Where carriers pad accessorials (and how to catch it)
- Contract validation: matching billed accessorials to contracted rates
- Automated dispute generation as a scaler
- ROI of auditing accessorials specifically

### Topic 4: "Contract vs. Spot Rate Divergence: How Billing Errors Track with Market Volatility"

**Angle:** Data/analytics  
**Key points:**
- Using SONAR data to identify high-risk periods for billing errors
- Correlation between rate volatility and invoice error frequency
- Why manual audit fails during market spikes
- How automated contract validation protects margin in volatile markets
- The broker's advantage: real-time audit data as a negotiation tool

### Topic 5: "Freight Audit Without Integration: Why the Best Tools Work With What You Already Have"

**Angle:** Counter-intuitive  
**Key points:**
- The myth that you need full TMS integration to automate audit
- EDGE77's approach: AI extraction from any invoice format
- How OCR + contract validation works without API access
- Samsara integration as a value-add (not a prerequisite)
- The path to TMS integration as you scale (Growth/Enterprise tiers)
