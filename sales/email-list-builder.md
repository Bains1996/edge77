## 6. How to Verify Emails

**Before sending, verify every email:**

1. **Syntax check** — Remove emails with invalid formats (missing @, spaces, etc.)
2. **Domain check** — Verify the domain has valid MX records (accepts mail)
3. **SMTP check** — Ping the mail server to confirm the mailbox exists (NeverBounce, ZeroBounce)
4. **Catch-all detection** — Catch-all domains (info@company.com) bounce at higher rates; score them lower
5. **Role account removal** — Strip info@, sales@, admin@, contact@ (decision makers require personal emails)

**Bounce threshold targets:**
- Good list: <2% bounce rate
- Acceptable list: 2–5% bounce rate
- Poor list: >5% bounce rate (reverify before sending)

## 9. Hygiene & Maintenance

- **Re-verify every 90 days** — Emails decay at ~2.5% per month
- **Remove hard bounces immediately** — Tag as invalid in CRM
- **Track engagement** — Score leads by opens, clicks, and replies
- **Warm new domains** — Send 5–10 emails/day ramping up to 100+/day over 2 weeks
- **SPF/DKIM/DMARC** — Ensure your sending domain is authenticated before any campaigns
