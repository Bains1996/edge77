## Sending Guidelines

**Timing:**
- Cold outreach: Send Tuesday–Thursday, 8:00–10:00 AM recipient's local time
- Demo follow-ups: Send within 24 hours of demo, then space by 2–3 days
- Re-engagement: Send Tuesday–Wednesday, mid-morning
- Partner outreach: Send Tuesday–Thursday, business hours

**Personalization tips:**
- Reference the recipient's specific freight volume or spend if available
- Mention their company name in the first sentence for cold emails
- Use industry-specific examples (manufacturing, retail, CPG, automotive)
- For CFOs, emphasize cost savings and ROI. For logistics managers, emphasize time savings and carrier relationship protection

**Compliance:**
- Include unsubscribe link in all emails
- Respect CAN-SPAM, GDPR, and CASL requirements
- Log all sends in CRM for tracking

**A/B testing recommendations:**
- Subject line variants: Test curiosity vs. specificity (e.g., "Last question" vs. "$127K recovered")
- CTA variants: Test "Run Free Audit" vs. "Get Recovery Estimate" vs. "Book a Demo"
- Sender name: Test personal name vs. "EDGE77 Team"
- Preview text: Test stat-driven vs. question-driven
