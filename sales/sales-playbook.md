## 2. Objection Handling — Top 10

### Objection 1: "We already audit our invoices manually."

**Response:** "That's great — most companies don't. But manual audits typically catch less than 30% of overcharges because they're reactive, not systematic. EDGE77's AI cross-references every line item against your contracted rates. One client who thought they had clean billing found $127K in overcharges in Q1. What if you're missing 70%?"

### Objection 2: "We trust our carriers."

**Response:** "I understand — most overcharges aren't malicious. Carrier billing systems are automated, and errors slip through. Wrong tariff applied, duplicate invoice generated, fuel surcharge miscalculated. It's not about trust — it's about verification. EDGE77 works behind the scenes; your carrier relationships stay intact."

### Objection 3: "We don't have the budget."

**Response:** "We don't require a budget — our Starter plan is $0/month. We work on a 15% contingency: we only get paid when we recover money for you. If we find nothing, you pay nothing. How is that for budget-friendly?"

### Objection 4: "Our TMS already does this."

**Response:** "Most TMS platforms offer basic audit capabilities — usually rules-based logic that catches maybe 1–2% of errors. EDGE77 uses AI-powered extraction and contract validation that catches 3–5% of spend. We're a complement, not a replacement. We integrate via API — no disruption to your existing workflow."

### Objection 5: "We're too small for this."

**Response:** "Actually, smaller companies often benefit the most because they don't have dedicated audit teams. Our Starter plan is $0/month and handles up to 50 invoices. If you're processing even 20 invoices a month, you could be losing $500–$2,000/month to errors. That's real money for a business your size."

### Objection 6: "We already use a freight audit provider."

**Response:** "Which provider? [Listen]. Many legacy providers charge monthly minimums or use manual processes. EDGE77 is AI-first with 99.7% extraction accuracy. We'd be happy to do a benchmark audit — run 100 of your invoices through both systems and compare results. If we don't find more, we shake hands and move on."

### Objection 7: "It will disrupt our carrier relationships."

**Response:** "EDGE77 operates entirely behind the scenes. We analyze invoices, flag discrepancies, and generate dispute letters. Carriers don't know we exist unless you want them to. We also recover funds diplomatically — our goal is recovery, not confrontation."

### Objection 8: "We have too much on our plate right now."

**Response:** "I get it — you're busy. That's actually why EDGE77 exists. We handle the entire audit process so your team doesn't have to. Forward invoices to us, and we do the rest. If we find overcharges, we file disputes. If we don't, you never hear from us. How much effort is that on your end?"

### Objection 9: "Send me information and I'll look at it."

**Response:** "Happy to send an overview. But I'd rather make this useful for you: I'll include a quick ROI estimate based on your invoice volume. If I send you a one-pager showing roughly what we'd recover for your company, would you take a 15-minute call to review it together?"

### Objection 10: "We need to think about it."

**Response:** "Completely fair. What specific questions do you need answered before making a decision? If it helps, I can set up a no-commitment free audit of 10 of your invoices right now. The results will make the decision much easier — one way or the other."

## 3. Pricing Scripts

### Starter Plan ($0/mo, 15% contingency)

**Script:**
"The Starter plan is completely free — no monthly fee, no credit card. You forward us your carrier invoices, our AI audits them, and we charge 15% of whatever we recover. If we find nothing, you pay nothing. Most companies find the Starter plan covers their needs for the first 3–6 months until they scale past 50 invoices/month."

**Who it's for:** Small brokers (<50 invoices/mo), companies wanting to test the service with zero risk.

### Growth Plan ($499/mo, 12% contingency)

**Script:**
"The Growth plan is $499/month with a reduced 12% contingency. You get up to 500 invoices/month, API access, Samsara integration, priority support, and advanced analytics. At this volume, the $499 fee is usually covered by the savings from a single invoice error. Most growing brokerages and logistics teams start here."

**Who it's for:** Mid-market companies (50–500 invoices/mo), teams that need API/Samsara integration.

### Enterprise Plan ($2,999/mo, 10% contingency)

**Script:**
"Enterprise is $2,999/month with a 10% contingency, unlimited invoices, dedicated account manager, custom audit rules, custom integrations, and an SLA guarantee. For companies processing thousands of invoices, the lowest contingency rate and highest support level pay for themselves. Most of our enterprise clients recover $50K–$500K+ in the first year."

**Who it's for:** Large 3PLs and shippers (500+ invoices/mo), companies needing custom rules and integrations.

### Concession Script (if price objection persists)

"If budget is the concern, let me suggest a phased approach. Start on the Starter plan — $0/month — and run a free audit on a sample of your invoices. If we find significant overcharges, you can upgrade to Growth when the volume justifies it. There's zero risk in starting."

## 4. Demo Script Outline

### Pre-Demo Checklist
- [ ] Confirm attendee is a decision maker (or has budget authority in the call)
- [ ] Confirm the current invoice volume and approximate freight spend
- [ ] Ask about existing audit process (if any)
- [ ] Ask about TMS/ERP/WMS system
- [ ] Ask about top 3 carriers
- [ ] Identify the primary pain point driving the demo request

### Demo Structure (20 minutes)

#### 0:00–2:00 — Introduction & Agenda
- "Thanks for the time [NAME]. Here's what I'll cover in 15 minutes:
  1. How EDGE77 works (2 min)
  2. Live product walkthrough (8 min)
  3. What recovery would look like for [COMPANY] (3 min)
  4. Next steps (2 min)"

#### 2:00–4:00 — Problem Reframe
- "Before I show the product, let me ask: how do you currently audit carrier invoices?"
- "What does your team do when they find a discrepancy?"
- "What would it mean for [COMPANY] to automate this entirely?"
- (Let them describe the pain — don't assume it.)

#### 4:00–12:00 — Product Walkthrough

*Show these screens in order:*

1. **Dashboard** — Total audits, overcharges found, recovery amount, fees earned
   - "This is what every client sees — real-time visibility into every audit."

2. **Invoice Upload / Email Forward** — "Forward any PDF invoice to audit@edge77.com"
   - Show the simplicity: no portal login needed, just forward an email.

3. **AI Extraction Output** — Show the 19+ extracted fields: base rate, fuel surcharge, accessorials, freight class, weight, etc.
   - "99.7% accuracy — Claude 3.5 Sonnet powers the extraction."

4. **Contract Rules Engine** — Show how to set fuel cap, accessorial limits, rate schedules
   - "Every charge is checked against your contracted rates. Nothing slips through."

5. **Audit Results** — Show flagged overcharges with dollar amounts
   - Highlight a specific overcharge (e.g., fuel surcharge 5.2% vs contracted 4.5%)

6. **Dispute Generation** — Show one-click dispute letter creation
   - "Legal-grade dispute letters with all findings documented. Approve and send."

7. **Samsara Integration** — Show connected data if relevant
   - "Cross-reference telematics data against invoices for detention and mileage verification."

#### 12:00–15:00 — ROI Calculation (Customized)

*Plug their data into this framework:*

```
Their Annual Freight Spend: $X
Industry Error Rate: 2–5%
Projected Annual Overcharges: $Y ($X × 2–5%)
EDGE77 Contingency (15%/12%/10%): $Z
Net Recovery for [COMPANY]: $Y – $Z
```

- "Based on what you shared, [COMPANY] is likely leaving $Y on the table every year. With EDGE77, you'd recover roughly $Y – $Z in the first year."

#### 15:00–18:00 — Q&A

Handle objections using the objection handling section above. Common demo objections:

- "How long does implementation take?" → "Days, not weeks. Forward invoices and you're live."
- "Do we need IT involvement?" → "No. Email forwarding or API integration — both are self-serve."
- "Can we try it before committing?" → "Absolutely. Starter plan is $0/month. No commitment."

#### 18:00–20:00 — Next Steps & Close

- "Based on what we discussed, I'd recommend starting on the [Starter/Growth/Enterprise] plan."
- "Here's what I'll do: send you a customized ROI summary and a link to start a free audit."
- "Can I send a DocuSign for the [Starter/Growth/Enterprise] plan?"
- "Let's schedule a 30-minute follow-up next week to review your first batch of audited invoices."

### Post-Demo Checklist
- [ ] Send thank-you email within 2 hours
- [ ] Attach custom ROI one-pager
- [ ] Send calendar link for implementation call (if sold)
- [ ] Add to CRM with deal stage and next action date
- [ ] Tag with persona and follow-up sequence

## 5. Closing Techniques

### Technique 1: The Assumptive Close

Use when the prospect has engaged positively.

"You mentioned catching fuel surcharge overcharges is a priority. Let's start you on the Starter plan today — I'll send you the onboarding link. You forward your first batch of invoices and we'll have results within 48 hours. Sound good?"

### Technique 2: The ROI Close

Use when the prospect is analytical.

"You're spending $X/year on freight. Industry data says 2–5% is overcharges. Even at the low end, that's $Y in recoverable money. Our contingency is 15% — so you keep $Z. Is there any reason not to start recovering that money?"

### Technique 3: The Risk Reversal Close

Use when the prospect hesitates on commitment.

"We're so confident EDGE77 will find overcharges in your invoices that I'll make you this offer: sign up for the Growth plan, and if we don't find at least $5,000 in overcharges in your first month, we'll refund your first month's fee. If we do find overcharges, you pay nothing more than the contingency. Fair?"

### Technique 4: The Limited-Time Close

Use in Email 3 (break-up email) or end-of-quarter.

"I have capacity to onboard 3 more clients this month at the Starter rate. After that, the $0/month offer remains available, but onboarding priority goes to paid plans. Want me to lock in a slot for [COMPANY]?"

### Technique 5: The Soft Close

Use with price-sensitive prospects.

"No pressure at all. Start on the free Starter plan. Audit your invoices for 30 days. If you see value, upgrade. If not, you're out nothing. Can I send you the setup link?"

## 8. Territory & Account Planning

### Ideal Account Profile Checklist

- [ ] Ships LTL or TL freight (not just parcel)
- [ ] Processes 50+ carrier invoices per month
- [ ] Has multiple carrier relationships
- [ ] Freight spend >$500K/year
- [ ] Has a logistics manager or dedicated AP for freight
- [ ] Uses a TMS or ERP (integration-ready)
- [ ] Not currently using an AI-powered audit provider
- [ ] Decision maker is accessible via email/LinkedIn

### Competitive Displacement Strategy

If they're using a competitor (Cass, CTSI-Global, RateLinx, Intelligent Audit, Trax, nVision Global):

1. **Find the gap:** "What's the #1 thing you wish your current audit provider did better?"
2. **Highlight AI advantage:** "Most legacy providers use rules-based or manual approaches. EDGE77 uses Claude 3.5 Sonnet AI with 99.7% extraction accuracy."
3. **Emphasize pricing:** "No monthly minimums. No long-term contracts. $0/month starter plan."
4. **Offer a benchmark:** "Let's run 100 of your past invoices through EDGE77 and compare findings."
5. **Reduce switching risk:** "We handle migration — upload your historical data and we do the rest."
