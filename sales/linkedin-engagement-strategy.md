# EDGE77 — LinkedIn Engagement & Growth Strategy

**Goal:** Grow followers, build authority, and generate leads for an AI-powered freight invoice audit platform
**Stage:** Early-stage (honest about it)
**Pricing Model:** $0/mo contingency pricing

## 1. Daily/Weekly Posting Schedule

### Weekly Content Cadence

| Day | Post Type | Format | Goal |
|-----|-----------|--------|------|
| Monday | Educational / Product Tip | Carousel or single image | Provide immediate value |
| Tuesday | Thought Leadership | Text + image | Build authority, provoke discussion |
| Wednesday | Educational Deep Dive | Infographic or long-form text | Position as expert |
| Thursday | Industry Stats / Data | Data graphic or chart | Credibility, shareability |
| Friday | Engagement (Poll / Story / Meme) | Interactive | Comments, shares, reach |

**Volume:** 5 posts/week (Mon-Fri). No weekend posts for B2B freight audience.

### Daily Execution Checklist

**Morning (8:00-8:30 AM ET)**
- Check notifications, respond to comments on yesterday's post
- Engage with 5-10 logistics influencers' posts (meaningful comments, not "Great post!")
- Share 1 relevant industry article from your feed (with 1-2 sentence commentary)

**Post Time (9:00 AM-12:00 PM ET)**
- Publish scheduled post
- First 60 minutes: reply to every comment immediately (boosts algorithm)

**Afternoon (3:00-4:00 PM ET)**
- Engage in 2-3 LinkedIn Groups (see groups list below)
- Send 5 connection requests with personalized notes
- Comment on 3-5 more posts from target audience

**Evening (optional, 8:00-9:00 PM ET)**
- Check for late comments, respond
- Queue next day's post

## 2. Commenting Strategy on Logistics Influencers

### Why This Matters
LinkedIn's algorithm rewards early, meaningful comments on high-engagement posts. A thoughtful comment on a post with 10K+ views gets more visibility than your own post to 500 followers.

### Target Influencers (Follow & Engage Regularly)

| Name / Handle | Focus | Engagement Strategy |
|--------------|-------|-------------------|
| FreightWaves (@FreightWaves) | Industry news | Comment on freight rate/volume posts with data perspective |
| Adam Robinson (@adamrrobinson) | Logistics tech, marketing | Add broker-specific angle to tech discussions |
| Chris Jolly (@chrisjolly) | Freight broker education | Share real-world audit insights |
| Glenn Koepke (@glennkoepke) | Supply chain networks | Comment on billing/payment topics |
| Cathy Morrow Roberson (@cathyroberson) | Logistics analysis | Add cost-recovery perspective |
| Evan Armstrong (@evanarmstrong) | 3PL insights | Reference freight audit data points |
| Jason Miller (@jasonmiller) | Supply chain data | Engage with trucking/freight stats posts |

### Commenting Rules

1. **Add value, don't regurgitate.** "Great post!" adds nothing. Instead: "The data on accessorial fee inflation tracks with what we see in audits — carriers in the Southeast region are 23% more likely to pad liftgate fees."

2. **Be specific.** Reference numbers, percentages, or experiences. Specificity builds credibility.

3. **Don't pitch.** Never comment "EDGE77 can help with this." Instead, establish expertise through data. If someone replies, then you can mention your product.

4. **Be early.** Comment within 30-60 minutes of posting. Follow influencers and turn on notifications.

5. **Respond to replies.** If the influencer or others reply to your comment, keep the conversation going.

### Comment Templates

**Data add-on:**
> "The billing error rate jumps significantly when fuel surcharges are involved. In our audit data, ~18% of invoices with FSC line items have miscalculations — typically wrong base rate or MPG assumption. It's the most overlooked line item in freight audit."

**Broker perspective:**
> "The challenge for brokerages is that manual audit catches obvious duplicates but misses carrier-level patterns — like a carrier that overcharges on residential delivery fees 12% of the time. That's where the real leakage lives."

**Reinforce with stat:**
> "Industry average is 15-20% of invoices with errors. Most brokerages we talk to think theirs is under 5%. The gap between perception and reality is where the money's hiding."

## 5. DM Outreach Templates

### Template 1: Warm Introduction (Connection Request Note)

**Subject:** Following your work in logistics

> Hi [Name], I've been following [their recent post/topic] and your perspective on [specific topic] stood out. I'm building EDGE77 — an AI-powered freight invoice audit platform. Not here to sell, just want to connect with thoughtful people in the space.

**Use when:** Connecting cold. 300 character limit on connection requests.

### Template 2: Value-First (After Connecting)

**Subject:** Quick question about freight audit

> Hey [Name], thanks for connecting. I noticed you work in [freight brokerage / 3PL / logistics].

> Quick question — are you handling freight invoice audits in-house, or do you have a tool for it?

> I ask because we built EDGE77 specifically for this — it's AI-powered and catches the billing errors manual review misses. $0/month to start, so no risk to try.

> Happy to share what we're seeing across the industry if you're curious. Either way, glad to be connected.

**Use when:** After they accept. Wait 2-3 days.

### Template 3: Insight Share (No Pitch)

**Subject:** Saw your post about [topic]

> Hey [Name], really liked your post about [topic]. Your point about [specific detail] especially resonated.

> We've been analyzing freight invoice data across [X] carrier invoices and seeing a surprising pattern: [specific insight, e.g., "fuel surcharge errors are 3x more common than duplicate charges but get far less attention"].

> Thought you might find that data point useful for your content. Happy to share more if helpful.

> No pitch — just sharing what we're seeing.

**Use when:** You want to build relationship without selling. Highest success rate for long-term trust.

### Template 4: Direct Offer (Warm Lead)

**Subject:** Free freight audit — no strings

> Hey [Name], following up on our connection. We're doing a limited round of free freight invoice audits for brokerages.

> Here's what we offer: send us 100 of your carrier invoices, and we'll run them through EDGE77's AI engine. You get a full report showing exactly what errors we found and how much you're overpaying. No cost, no sales call.

> Most brokerages discover 3-7% in overcharges they didn't know about. If you're curious about what's hiding in your invoices, happy to run the audit.

> Interested?

**Use when:** They've engaged with your content or you've had a prior conversation.

### Template 5: Group Member Outreach

**Subject:** Saw you in [Group Name]

> Hey [Name], noticed we're both in the [Group Name] group. I really liked your comment on [specific thread/topic].

> I'm one of the founders of EDGE77 — we do AI-powered freight invoice audit. Given your background in [their expertise], I'd love to get your take on something:

> [Specific question about their opinion on an industry trend]

> Would value your perspective. No sales pitch — genuinely curious what someone with your experience thinks.

**Use when:** You've seen them active in a group. Personalize heavily.

### DM Best Practices

- **Never send the template verbatim.** Personalize at least 1-2 sentences.
- **Wait 48-72 hours** between connection acceptance and outreach.
- **Call-to-action:** One clear question or offer per message.
- **Follow-up:** If no reply in 5-7 days, send one brief follow-up. Then stop.
- **Volume:** Max 5-10 DMs/day. Quality over quantity.

## 6. Paid LinkedIn Ad Strategy

### Budget Recommendations

| Phase | Monthly Budget | Objective | Duration |
|-------|---------------|-----------|----------|
| Testing | $300-500/mo | Website visits + engagement | Month 1-2 |
| Scaling | $500-1,500/mo | Lead generation | Month 3+ |
| Retargeting | $200-500/mo | Content downloads, demo requests | Month 3+ |

### Campaign 1: Awareness (Website Traffic)

**Objective:** Drive traffic to EDGE77 website or landing page
**Budget:** $300/month

**Targeting:**
- Location: United States
- Job Titles: Freight Broker, 3PL Owner, Logistics Manager, Supply Chain Director, VP Logistics, Transportation Manager
- Company Size: 11-200 employees (sweet spot for mid-size brokerages)
- Industries: Transportation/Trucking/Railroad, Logistics and Supply Chain, Freight and Package Delivery
- Exclude: Students, job seekers, employees of EDGE77 competitors

**Ad Format:** Single image or document ad (PDF carousel)
**Creative:** "Is your freight audit catching everything? Find out in 10 minutes."
**Landing Page:** Dedicated page with free audit offer (collect email)

### Campaign 2: Lead Generation (Message Ad)

**Objective:** Generate qualified leads via direct messaging
**Budget:** $500/month

**Targeting:** Same as above, narrowed to:
- Seniority: Owner, Partner, C-Suite, Director, VP
- More than 1 year in current position

**Ad Format:** LinkedIn Message Ad (sent to InMail)
**Message:**

> Subject: Free freight invoice audit from EDGE77
>
> Hi [Name],
>
> We're offering a free AI-powered audit of 100 carrier invoices — no cost, no strings.
>
> You'll receive a detailed report showing:
> • How many billing errors you have
> • How much you're overpaying
> • Which carriers overcharge most
>
> Most brokerages find 3-7% in hidden overcharges.
>
> Want to see what's in your invoices?
>
> [CTA Button: Claim Free Audit]

**Expected Cost Per Lead:** $40-80 (typical for logistics B2B on LinkedIn)

### Campaign 3: Retargeting (Content Engagement)

**Objective:** Convert website visitors who didn't take action
**Budget:** $200/month

**Audience:** Website visitors (LinkedIn Insight Tag required)
- Visited website but didn't sign up
- Viewed pricing page
- Viewed blog/content page

**Ad Format:** Single image with testimonial stat
**Creative:** "See what other brokerages found in their audit. One team recovered $28K in month one."
**Landing Page:** Case study or demo booking

### Campaign 4: Sponsored Content (Thought Leadership)

**Objective:** Build authority and follower count
**Budget:** $300-500/month

**Content to Boost:**
- Posts with highest organic engagement (day 2-3 after posting)
- Customer story posts (highest converting content)
- Data-driven posts (best for credibility)

**Boost Strategy:**
- Spend $20-30/post to test
- Kill campaigns spending >$5/click (reenable with different creative)
- Scale winners to $50-100/day for 3-5 days

### Top 5 Ad Creatives (Test These)

1. **"1 in 5 freight invoices has an error."** — Bold stat, simple design
2. **"We audit your invoices for free."** — Direct offer, low risk
3. **"Is your carrier overcharging you?"** — Question format, high click-through
4. **"Most brokers leave 3-7% of freight spend on the table."** — Pain point + stat
5. **"AI for freight audit. $0/mo. No catch."** — Honest, clear value prop

### Targeting Exclusions

To avoid wasted spend, exclude:
- Students
- Freelancers
- Employees of: freight carriers (they're not buyers), direct competitors
- Job titles: Driver, Warehouse Worker, Dispatcher (unless they also have management titles)

### Measurement & Optimization

| Metric | Target | Action if Below |
|--------|--------|----------------|
| Click-through rate (CTR) | >0.5% | Refresh creative, test new headline |
| Cost per click (CPC) | <$8 | Narrow targeting, improve relevance |
| Cost per lead (CPL) | <$80 | Improve landing page, test offer |
| Message ad response rate | >15% | Refine messaging, adjust targeting |
| Follower acquisition cost | <$5 | Test different creative angles |

### LinkedIn Insight Tag Setup

Install the Insight Tag on:
- `https://www.edge77.com/` (all pages)
- Blog or resource pages
- Pricing page
- Free audit landing page

This enables retargeting and conversion tracking.

## 7. 90-Day Engagement Roadmap

### Month 1: Foundation
- Set up company page (see profile optimization guide)
- Founders optimize personal profiles
- Begin 5x/week posting schedule
- Join 10 LinkedIn Groups, observe only
- Send 20 connection requests/day (600 total)

- Begin group posting (2-3 posts/week in top 3 groups)
- Start influencer engagement strategy
- Launch first LinkedIn Ads campaign ($300/mo testing)
- Send DM outreach templates 2-4 to warm connections
- **Goal:** 1,500 followers, 10-15 qualified conversations

### Month 3: Scale
- Scale ad spend to $1,000+/mo
- Retargeting campaign active
- Begin customer story content from real users
- Evaluate top-performing post types, double down
- **Goal:** 3,000+ followers, 5+ qualified leads/month
