# EDGE77 — Billion-Dollar Roadmap

## THE MATH

**Current state:** Working AI freight audit SaaS, live on Cloud Run, $0 revenue.

**Target:** $1B+ valuation in 3-5 years.

**How freight audit works:**
- US freight market: $900B+ annually
- Billing error rate: 2-5% of all invoices
- That's $18-45B in overcharges per year
- Recovery firms take 15-50% of recovered amount
- EDGE77 takes 15% = $2.7-6.7B addressable market

**To hit $100M ARR (necessary for $1B valuation):**
- At 15% contingency, need ~$667M in recovered overcharges
- Average overcharge per invoice: $200-500
- Need to process 1.3-3.3M invoices/year
- At 100 invoices/client/month, need 1,100-2,800 clients
- At $49-149/month subscription + 15% contingency, realistic ARR: $100M+
