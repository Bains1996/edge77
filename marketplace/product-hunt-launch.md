## Description (Long — for PH page)

### The Problem

Every year, $4.2 billion is lost to freight carrier overcharges. 87% of shippers never audit their invoices because it's too time-consuming and complex.

### The Solution

EDGE77 is an AI-powered freight invoice audit platform that:

🔍 **Audits in 30 seconds** — Upload a PDF, and our AI scans every line item
🚨 **Catches every error** — Freight class manipulation, DIM weight overcharges, invalid accessorials, duplicate charges
⚡ **Auto-files disputes** — We generate and submit carrier claims automatically
💰 **Recovers your money** — Track savings on a real-time dashboard

### How It Works

1. Upload your freight invoice (PDF, email, or EDI)
2. AI audits every line against carrier contracts and tariffs
3. Errors are flagged with detailed explanations
4. Disputes are auto-filed with carriers
5. You get your money back

### Who It's For

- **Freight Shippers** — Companies spending $10K+/month on freight
- **Freight Brokers** — Audit carrier invoices before paying
- **3PLs** — Add audit services to your offering
