## Targeting Checklist

### Ideal Customer Profile (ICP)
- **Company type:** Freight broker, 3PL, shipper, manufacturer
- **Invoice volume:** 100+ LTL invoices/month
- **Geography:** North America (US, Canada, Mexico)
- **Technology:** Uses Samsara, KeepTruckin, or similar ELD
- **Pain points:** Manual audit process, billing disputes, carrier issues

### Where to Find Leads
1. **LinkedIn Sales Navigator** — Search for "freight broker," "3PL," "logistics manager"
2. **FreightWaves SONAR** — Identify high-volume lanes and carriers
3. **Freight broker directories** — FMCSA database, DAT, Truckstop.com
4. **Industry events** — TCA, CSCMP, IPC events
5. **Cold email tools** — Apollo.io, Hunter.io, Lemlist
