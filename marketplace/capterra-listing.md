## Description (1500 chars)

EDGE77 is an AI-powered freight invoice audit and dispute platform that helps shippers, freight brokers, and 3PLs recover money lost to carrier billing errors.

Every year, $4.2 billion is lost to freight overcharges. 87% of shippers never audit their invoices. EDGE77 changes that with AI that scans every line of every invoice in seconds — catching freight class manipulation, DIM weight overcharges, invalid accessorials, and duplicate charges.

**How It Works:**
1. Upload invoices (PDF, email, or EDI)
2. AI scans and audits every line item
3. Errors are flagged with explanations
4. Disputes are auto-filed with carriers
5. Track savings on your dashboard
