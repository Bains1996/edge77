- **AI Invoice Audit:** Automatically audits LTL and TL invoices against tariff rates, detecting accessorial charges, weight reclassifications, duplicate billing, and fuel surcharge errors
- **Samsara ELD Integration:** Connects directly to fleet management systems for automatic rate benchmarking and shipment verification
- **Automated Dispute Letters:** Generates carrier dispute letters with supporting documentation, reducing claim resolution time from weeks to days
- **Financial Dashboard:** Real-time tracking of detected overcharges, recovery rates, and P&L impact
- **Multi-Carrier Support:** Works across all major LTL and TL carriers with continuously updated tariff databases
