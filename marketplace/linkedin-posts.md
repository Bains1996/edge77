## Post 4: Educational — How to Read a Freight Bill

**Post Type:** Carousel (5 slides)

**Content:**
Most shippers don't know how to read a freight bill. Here's a 60-second guide:

Slide 1: How to Read a Freight Bill in 60 Seconds

Slide 2: Check the NMFC Code
Verify the freight class matches your actual product. Carriers often upgrade class to charge more.

Slide 3: Verify DIM Weight
Compare carrier's calculation vs actual: L×W×H ÷ 139. Carriers charge the HIGHER of actual vs DIM weight.

Slide 4: Review Accessorials
Was liftgate actually used? Was there a delivery appointment? Were these services requested?

Slide 5: Match BOL to Invoice
Weight, class, and accessorial codes must match the Bill of Lading.

Slide 6: Let AI Handle It
EDGE77 checks all of this automatically. 30 seconds. Every invoice. Every time.
