### Stop Overpaying on Freight Invoices

EDGE77 is an AI-powered freight invoice audit and dispute platform built for shippers, freight brokers, and 3PLs. Our AI scans every line of your freight invoices to catch billing errors, overcharges, and discrepancies that cost businesses billions every year.

### The Problem We Solve

87% of shippers don't audit their freight invoices. Carrier billing errors — including freight class manipulation, DIM weight overcharges, invalid accessorials, and duplicate charges — cost the industry $4.2 billion annually. Most companies don't have the time or expertise to catch these errors manually.

### How EDGE77 Works

1. **Upload Your Invoices** — PDF, email, or EDI. We accept any format.
2. **AI Scans Every Line** — Our AI checks freight class, DIM weight, accessorials, and more against your contracts and carrier tariffs.
3. **Catch Errors Automatically** — We flag every overcharge with detailed explanations.
4. **File Disputes For You** — Our system automatically generates and files dispute claims with carriers.
5. **Track Your Savings** — Dashboard shows real-time recovery amounts and audit analytics.

### Key Features

- **AI-Powered Audit Engine** — GPT-4 powered analysis catches errors humans miss
- **Multi-Carrier Support** — Works with all major LTL and FTL carriers
- **Automated Dispute Filing** — Generate and submit carrier claims automatically
- **Real-Time Dashboard** — Track savings, audit status, and recovery amounts
- **Contract Compliance** — Verify carrier charges against your negotiated rates
- **DIM Weight Validation** — Catch dimensional weight calculation errors
- **Accessorial Audit** — Verify liftgate, delivery appointment, and other accessorial charges
- **Multi-Tenant** — Manage multiple shippers from one platform
- **API Integration** — Connect with TMS, ERP, and WMS systems
- **SOC 2 Compliant** — Enterprise-grade security
